About Us

We started as a small group of people who wanted to use our love of gaming to be a connecting point for other students. In college, there is so much for students to stress about in their time. Students have assignments, exams, grades, interships, and future careers to worry about at all time of day. This website will act as hub that allows students to better connect with one another over an interest in gaming and to relieve some of that stress. Here's to building life-long freindships and making connections through an activity that so many people adore.

DISCLAIMER

Rutgers gamers is a group ran by Rutgers students, for students. Rutgers Gamers does not make any profit from its members or linked clubs in anyway and never will.
